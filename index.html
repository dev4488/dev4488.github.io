Head, ML Systems Engineering, TD bank (Layer6)
Ex-Postdoc Stanford University

Current head of ML Systems Engineering at TD Bank. ML engineering & research leader specializing in building & operationalizing ML models in complex production environments. Looking to build/scale machine learning team(s) & operations to deliver business value. Before this I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at Stanford University. My research centered around Deep Learning and its application in Computer Vision, multi sensor fusion & Explainable AI.

I obtained my PhD at VIP lab - University of Waterloo and Machine Learning Research Group - University of Guelph where I was supervised by Dr. Alexander Wong (UWaterloo/ Canada research chair, Medical Imaging & AI) and Dr. Graham Taylor (UGuelph/ Academic Direction, Vector Inst./ Canada research chair, ML). During my PhD I also developed various PoC projects for different industry clients via UWaterloo partnership & independent projects.

In past, I was the Lead AI Scientist for NextAI, a world class AI accelerator program of NEXT Canada based in Toronto, Canada. There I lead a team of AI scientists that provided AI R&D advice to various startups.

In distant past, I was a deep learning research intern at Philips research (HQ), a research engineer in LIP-6 - UPMC-Sorbonne University, Paris. There I had great time working with Prof. Matthieu Cord and Asst. Prof Nicolas Thome
