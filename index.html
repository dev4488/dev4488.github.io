Devinder Kumar devinder.kumar@uwaterloo.ca

My research focuses on creating image analysis tools using deep learning architectures for classification and recognition, mainly in the field of cancer imaging and mobile robotics.

I am a first year PhD student at VIP lab at University of Waterloo. I work with Dr. Alexander Wong and Dr. Graham Taylor. Before this, I completed my MASc.in WAVE lab & VIP lab at University of Waterloo. where I worked with Asst. Prof Steven Waslander and Prof. David Clausi.

Previous to joining UWaterloo, I was a research engineer in LIP-6 at UPMC-Sorbonne University. There I had great time working with Prof. Matthieu Cord and Asst. Prof Nicolas Thome
