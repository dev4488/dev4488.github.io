Devinder Kumar devinder.kumar@uwaterloo.ca

I develop computer vision tools to analyze Image data. This includes object identification/classification, motion tracking and mobile robot vision.

As of Winter 2015, I am a graduate student in WAVE lab & VIP lab at University of Waterloo. I work with Asst. Prof Steven Waslander and Prof. David Clausi.

Before this, I was a research engineer in LIP-6 at UPMC-Sorbonne University. There I had great time working with Prof. Matthieu Cord and Asst. Prof Nicolas Thome
